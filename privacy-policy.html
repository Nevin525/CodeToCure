Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Code to Cure KCNH5 Epilepsy Foundation ("we," "us," or "the Foundation") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or support our mission, how we use it, and the choices you have. We collect as little personal information as possible and never sell it.

Information We Collect

  • Email sign-ups. If you choose to join our mailing list, we collect the email address (and any name or details) you provide so we can send updates about the Foundation's work.
  • Donations. When you make a donation, you provide payment and contact information through our donation platform, Zeffy. Payment card details are handled directly by Zeffy and its payment processors, so we never see or store your full card number.
  • Website analytics. We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand general site usage. This is privacy-focused, cookieless, and collects only aggregated, non-identifying data such as page views, referring websites, country, browser, and device type.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send newsletters, updates, and news about the Foundation (only if you signed up).
  • To process and acknowledge your donations, including issuing receipts.
  • To understand, in aggregate, how visitors use our site so we can improve it.
  • To comply with our legal, tax, and accounting obligations as a nonprofit.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use tracking cookies for analytics. Cloudflare Web Analytics runs without cookies and does not build a profile of you or follow you across other websites. Cloudflare may process your IP address to provide this service, but it is not stored by us or made available to us in identifiable form.

Donations and Payments (Zeffy)

Donations are processed through Zeffy, a third-party donation platform. When you donate, the information you enter is collected and processed by Zeffy under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review Zeffy's Privacy Policy. We receive donation records (such as your name, email, and donation amount) so we can thank you, issue receipts, and keep accurate records.

Third-Party Services and Links

Our website links to our social media profiles and uses third-party services to operate. These include:

  • Zeffy: donation and email sign-up processing.
  • Cloudflare: website hosting/performance and privacy-focused analytics.
  • Social media platforms: we link to our profiles on Instagram, Facebook, X, TikTok, and LinkedIn. When you click through to these sites, their own privacy policies apply.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites and recommend reviewing their policies.

How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share information only with the service providers listed above (acting on our behalf to operate the site, process donations, and send email), or when required by law.

Data Retention

We keep donor and mailing-list information only as long as needed for the purposes described here or as required by law (for example, tax recordkeeping). Cloudflare Web Analytics data is retained for approximately six months. You can ask us to remove your information at any time.

Your Choices and Rights

  • Unsubscribe. Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt out at any time.
  • Access, correction, or deletion. You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it.
  • EU/UK visitors. If you are in the European Economic Area or the UK, you have rights under the GDPR, including access, rectification, erasure, and objection. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.
