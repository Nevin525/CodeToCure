Our Mission

We refuse to wait for a cure.

As a 501(c)(3) public charity in formation, our mission is to raise awareness for KCNH5-related genetic epilepsy and secure the donor support that accelerates research toward a cure.

We bring families, advocates, and donors together, turning a community's determination into real momentum for the science.

Born from one family's resolve, built for every family waiting.

The Science

Engineering a cure at the source.

KCNH5 mutation-related epilepsy is a rare, severe neurological disorder that causes early-onset seizures and developmental delays. We rally awareness and donor support behind targeted, translation-ready research that addresses the root cause, not just the symptoms.

01 / APPROACH

Antisense Oligonucleotide (ASO) therapeutics

Programmable genetic medicine designed to intercept the faulty instructions behind KCNH5 seizures and quiet them before they ever reach the cell, silencing the root cause rather than masking it.

Silence the signal
02 / APPROACH

CRISPR-based gene correction

Gene-correction models that work toward rewriting the mutated code itself, restoring healthy function where the disorder begins so therapies can reach the children waiting for them.

Rewrite the code
BENCH TO BEDSIDE

Bridging laboratory data and patient care.

Breakthroughs stall in the gap between the lab and the clinic. The donor funding we help secure moves that work forward, so promising science becomes a therapy a child can actually receive.

FOCUS

Translation-ready research only.

Every dollar raised is directed toward science with a credible path to patients.
