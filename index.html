Our Mission

We refuse to wait for a cure.

As an IRS-authorized 501(c)(3) public charity, Code to Cure KCNH5 Epilepsy Foundation exists to fund and accelerate groundbreaking research to find a cure for KCNH5 genetic epilepsy. We bring families, advocates, and donors together to amplify our impact. No family has to face this alone. Together, we can make the cure a reality.

What is KCNH5?

Accelerating Research to Save Lives.

KCNH5 epilepsy is a rare and severe condition. A single mutation within this gene can reshape a child's entire life with recurrent seizures and developmental delays. Understanding it is the first step toward correcting it.

What it is
Rare genetic epilepsy
When it starts
Early childhood
What it brings
Recurrent seizures, developmental delay
Why it happens
A mutation in the KCNH5 gene

Research

Two paths to a cure.

Your support helps fund research that goes after the root cause of KCNH5 epilepsy, not just the symptoms, so children have a real chance at a healthier future.

Approach 01

Antisense oligonucleotide (ASO) therapy

A targeted therapy designed to silence the mutated genetic instructions that trigger KCNH5 seizures, stopping the harmful signals at the source instead of masking symptoms.

Silence the signal
Approach 02

CRISPR gene correction

Research working toward permanently rewriting the mutated gene itself, restoring healthy function right at the source.

Rewrite the code
